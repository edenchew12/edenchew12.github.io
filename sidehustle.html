Side Hustles

Apart from technological development, I enjoying selling products through the e-Commerce space. This first started out as a way to earn side income but have since grew to be an avid interest of mine.

FreeFall Fitness

  • When gyms closed down during Circuit Breaker 2020 due to the Covid-19 pandemic, many pivoted towards bodyweight exercises to keep fit.
  • With that, me and my partner saw this as an opportunity to start selling Calisthenic Rings in hopes of motivating people to kickstart their fitness journey at home.
  • I designed the products myself utilizing SolidWorks as well as sourced for a manufacturer overseas to produce the product.
  • We were able to grow the business organically with the help of social media platforms and online e-Commerce store. We managed to gain profitability within a month after product launch.

Pomade Reseller (Carousell)

  • My first exposure running an online business / e-Commerce store.
  • I was an avid pomade (hairstyling product) collector.
  • The store started off as a way to clear excess pomade jars that I had purchased in bulk to reduce shipment cost.
  • The store slowly gained traction and profitability and I went on to bring in more pomades from independent home-brewers to resell to the local market.
